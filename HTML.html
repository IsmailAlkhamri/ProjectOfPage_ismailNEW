Types of Networks By dependening on Topologies (t):

  • LAN :
    Stands for Local Area Network .
    It is network that connects devices through limited area.
    It is used in small organizations and companies that have one building .
  • WAN :
    Stands for Wide Area Network .
    It is network that connects devices through large area.
    It is used in Large organizations that have more than one building or countries.
  • MAN :
    Stands for Wide Area Network .
    It is network that connects devices through large area like cities.
    It is smaller than WAN .
